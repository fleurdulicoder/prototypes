DIRECTIONS

1) Download and print a DD Form 2535 (NEW DD FORM 2535 AS OF DEC 2016)

2) Send the Form to your local Flight Standards District Office (FSDO) for approval by the FAA

3) Make sure the form is completely filled out then send it to
HQMC.COMREL@USMC.MIL with the date and location of the
event in the subject line
(i.e. Color Guard Request-25July2019-Southampton, New York)

Please do not mail or fax your completed DD Form 2535 since doing this will significantly increase processing time.

If you have any changes regarding the request after it has been submitted/deemed eligible (event dates, points of contact, emails, telephone numbers) by HQMC COMREL you must directly communicate that to the aviation liaison at HQMC.COMREL@USMC.MIL. Failure to report any changes from the original request to the Marine Corp's aviation liaison may result in aviation support for your event canceled.

If you need any additional assistance please call 703-614-1034
